Chicken Alfredo

Description

If you need a luxurious break from tomato-based sauces, this four-cheese chicken alfredo pasta bake delivers pure indulgence. It features tender bits of pre-cooked chicken breast and tube pasta coated in a silky, home-style garlic and cream cheese alfredo sauce, resulting in a velvety casserole that will satisfy any cheese lover.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne or rigatoni pasta
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or cubed
  • 1 (15 ounce) jar quality Alfredo sauce
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Provolone cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the pasta in boiling water according to package directions until just al dente, then drain.
  3. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and sauté the minced garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the softened cream cheese and Alfredo sauce, whisking gently until completely smooth and melted together.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cooked chicken pieces, and the warm Alfredo sauce mixture.
  6. Fold in the Provolone cheese, Parmesan cheese, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Pour the pasta mixture into the prepared baking dish and press down gently into an even layer.
  8. Scatter the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the top of the pasta.
  9. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes until the cheese topping turns a light golden brown and bubbles. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
